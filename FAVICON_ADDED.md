## 💡 Pro Tips

### **Optimize for Browser Tabs:**
Your current logo (251 KB PNG) works great! But for even faster loading:

**Option 1: Use .ico format (recommended for old browsers)**
- Convert PNG → ICO at: https://www.icoconverter.com
- Add: `<link rel="icon" href="images/favicon.ico">`

**Option 2: Multiple sizes (best practice)**
Create different sizes for different uses:
```html
<link rel="icon" type="image/png" sizes="32x32" href="images/favicon-32x32.png">
<link rel="icon" type="image/png" sizes="16x16" href="images/favicon-16x16.png">
<link rel="apple-touch-icon" sizes="180x180" href="images/apple-touch-icon-180x180.png">
```
